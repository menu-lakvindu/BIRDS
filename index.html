Birds can be broadly categorized in many ways, but one common distinction is between these two main types
1. Flying Birds (Carinates)
These birds have a keeled breastbone (carina) that supports strong flight muscles. Most birds fall into this category and include species like:
-Eagles
-Sparrows
-Parrots
-Pigeons
2. Flightless Birds (Ratites)
These birds lack a keeled breastbone and cannot fly. They are usually larger and adapted for running or swimming. Examples include:
-Ostriches
-Emus
-Kiwis
-Penguins
